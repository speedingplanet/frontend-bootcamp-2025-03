HTML Tables

Tables have this structure: 

<table>
	<thead>
		<tr>
			<th>Header 1</th>
			<th>Header 2</th>
			<th>Header 3</th>
		</tr>
	</thead>
	<tbody>
		<tr>
			<td>Row 1 Item 1</td>
			<td>Row 2 Item 2</td>
			<td>Row 3 Item 3</td>
		</tr>
		<tr>
			<td>Row 1 Item 1</td>
			<td>Row 2 Item 2</td>
			<td>Row 3 Item 3</td>
		</tr>
	</tbody>
</table>
